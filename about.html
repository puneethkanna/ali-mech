Due to heavy population, road congestion a nightmare to every city dweller. Be it a student at be it a working professional road congestions spare no one, during peak hours of the day, traffic moves at snail's pace. With the ever increasing number of urban population increasing number of owned vehicles and unfortunately constrained size of the roads, road congestions are in epidemic spreading across cities at high pace, Especially in metropolitan cities. Road congestions are a never ending tale and of course, everyone hates that Whe attending the complaint by Ambulance Services, it is becoming very difficult to reach the emergencies due to road congestions in metropolitan cities To overcome the problem, it is proposed to create Telangana Ambulance Project in one of our metropolitan city Hyderabad to serve the medical emergencies in the State of Telangana This service operated initially in cities of Telangana through call centers and will expand throughout the State in due course
